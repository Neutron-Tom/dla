DLA Simulation - Iframe Embedding Test

Testing iframe compatibility:

  • Responsive width (100%)
  • Fixed height (600px)
  • Touch and mouse controls
  • No external navigation

How to use:

  1. Click and drag on the black canvas to draw boundaries (cyan)
  2. Watch particles (yellow) spawn from the center and move with Brownian motion
  3. Particles stick to boundaries and turn pink when they collide
  4. Adjust speed and spawn rate with sliders
  5. Click Reset to start over
